About

I am a Machine Learning Researcher at RBC Borealis AI. I received my Ph.D. from Simon Fraser University (SFU), where I was advised by Prof. Yasutaka Furukawa. Prior to SFU, I completed my M.Sc. at Seoul National University (SNU) under the supervision of Prof. Cho Nam Ik, and my B.Sc. in Electrical and Computer Engineering at SNU.

My research interests include computer vision, geometric deep learning, anomaly detection, and time series forecasting.
